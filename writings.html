Project Enigma

Returning to school after recovering from a nearly-fatal car accident that claimed the life of a young child, John Novak finds himself a social outcast but something is different inside him, something strange. He keeps experiencing weird humming noises in his head, the lights flicker whenever he walks into a room and he finds men in suits following him at a distance. John discovers that something in the accident caused him to change, change into something extraordinary.

Gravestone Manor

Elias Rourke and his twin sister, Elise, are all that remain of the famous Rourke family of Havenrock. Following the disapperance on their parents, the twins are left with nothing more than their parents's journals and Gravestone Manor, their former home.

Now a mysterious killer walks the streets with the twin in their sights. Elias and Elise must come face-to-face with their family's secrets and realize that their world is much bigger than they thought.
